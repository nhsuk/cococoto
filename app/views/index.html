{% markdown %} # Coronavirus content for NHS products and services: Symptoms ## How we write about coronavirus symptoms There are 4 main ways we talk about symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19). These are designed so they can be used in different contexts and across a range of products and services. ### Short version: > The main symptoms of coronavirus (COVID-19) are a high temperature, a new, continuous cough and a loss or change to your sense of smell or taste. ### Expanded version: {{ expanded | safe }} ### Voice-friendly version: **What are the symptoms of coronavirus?** {{ voice | safe }} ### Full version: See our full page on the NHS website about [check if you have coronavirus symptoms](https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/check-if-you-have-coronavirus-symptoms/#symptoms). ### Where our content comes from Our symptoms content is based on official guidance published by Public Health England (PHE) and the office of the Chief Medical Officers (CMOs) of the UK, as well as content being used by the NHS 111 service. It was been adapted and simplified by our content team before being assured by our internal clinical team at NHS Digital, including our Chief Medical Officer. ## Sign up for alerts when we change our symptoms content If you want to adapt our symptoms content for your product or service, we an can alert you each time our content changes.
